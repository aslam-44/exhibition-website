SIGNUM 2022

In today’s era, Education is not only confined to the ability to read and write but is linked to the holistic development of the children. Education in India have come a long way with more children receiving quality education. Government schemes support and initiate education for all despite all differences with regard to class, caste, religion and so on. However, in contemporary digital world, the challenge and the demand is not only limited to having education, but it is more about how impeccably one can present that knowledge from education. Hence, the skill enhancement and on the field training becomes important for every child to become equipped to face the challenges. This is the reason why at National School we leave no stone unturned to help students to experiment with their knowledge and are providing them different opportunities to showcase their skills. These include exhibitions, seminars, projects, presentations, competitions, technical events, etc. SIGNUM 2022 is one of the initiative by team National to make our little buds more confident, creative, future-ready, and of course learners for life. Through SIGNUM 2022, National School aims to enhance the student’s knowledge and make learning a fun experience. These exhibitions also help to develop in students a more scientific attitude towards problems and challenges that they face in life. Students apply their classroom knowledge in these exhibitions and develop the taste for learning by doing. It shifts them from theoretical study to practical implementation and they avail the opportunity to apply their ideas in unique ways.
